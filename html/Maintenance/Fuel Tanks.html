The fuel tanks are of the wet-wing type with intermediate ribs serving as baffles. There is one tank in each wing. The inboard end of the fuel tank is closed by the solid rib at the wing root.

CAUTION

The use of poen flame, non-explosion-proof lighting, or power tools inside the fuel tanks or near fuel-tank openeings can cause fire, explosion, or both. When using a falshlight or explosion-proof lighting, DO NOT SWITCH THE LIGHT ON OR OFF WHILE INSIDE THE TANK.
