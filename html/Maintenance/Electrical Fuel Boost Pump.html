There are two common electric fuel boost pump installations

Aircraft prior to 2019 were equipped with a CECO 9135 pump and Delco A949 motor or alternatively with a Titan CH4103-B-23 pump and Pesco 13688 motor. The schematic for these installations is shown in Fig Cross Reference.
