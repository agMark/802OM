The oil filter, located to the right side of the air inlet case, is held in place by a cover with 4 nuts. Removing the oil filter requires a special puller and should not be removed by any other means. Cleaning the filter may be accomplished by gently agitating the element in clean solvent. After cleaning, allow the element to stand in a clean environment until dry. Refer to the proper Pratt and Whitney Manual in Cross Reference Placeholder.

CAUTION

Under no circumstances may the element be ultrasonically cleaned, pressure flushed, or dried with compressed air. Such cleaning and drying causes damage to the filter media.
