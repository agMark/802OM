Details of the elevator trim tabs are shown below in Fig Cross Reference. The elevator trim tabs are p/n 30600-1 L/H, 30600-2 R/H and attach to the elevators with a p/n 30610-1 hinge that bolts in the inboard rib with AN3-5A bolts and AN960-10 washers.

The trim tabs are shimmed laterally with shims. Lateral free play should be between .050 and .020 in. To lubricate the tab bushings remove the #8 machine screw. Oil the bushings through the screw hole and at the end of the tab. Use general purpose oil for this. Replace the #8 oil hole plug screw.
