Pitot/Static Calibration

The Altimeter uses a density altitude and compares the pressure to a measured static pressure from the static port.

Using Pitot Static tester attach porbe to pitot tube. Pitot tube on the AT-802A is under the left hand wing. For the two seater AT-802-2, the pitot and static probe is in the leading edge of the left hand wing. The probe on the AT-802-2 will require and adapter to connect vacuum port.
