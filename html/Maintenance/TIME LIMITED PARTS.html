To determine the life limits for airplane parts, the operator should review the following in their order of precedence:

  1. Airworthiness Directives (AD's)
  2. Airworthiness Limitations Section of this Manual
  3. Recommended Time Limits Section of this Manual
  4. Suggested Time Limits Section of this Manual

None of these recommendations or suggestions override the need for condition inspections and replacement of items should it be needed. If a required item is noticed to be in unserviceable condition prior to a recommended or suggested replacement interval, replace it!
