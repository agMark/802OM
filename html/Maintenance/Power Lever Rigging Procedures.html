Rig Engine Power Controls and Reversing System as follows:

  1. Disconnect reversing cable from propeller control cam on aft r/h side of engine. Move fork and cable assy up & inboard out of the way of the propeller control cam.
  2. Remove propeller reversing lever from fwd. end of push-pull cable assy. Do not lose the bushing (P&W p/n 3011554) in the top of the arm. Install Hartzell p/n A- 3044 carbon block in reversing arm.
  3. Install carbon block at end of reversing lever into the beta ring. Block should have .001 clearance. If not, use fine sandpaper on flat sanding surface, sanding a little off block until .001 clearance is obtained.
  4. Install the new P&W p/n 3011554 bushing on top of the propeller reversing arm and install bolt, nut, and cotter pin. Check the inside surface of the Beta valve clevis to see if it is flush with the fwd end of the Beta Valve nut. If not, adjust propeller governor interconnect rod until it is flush. Governor Air Bleed Link Arm must be against stud. Torque jam nuts safety with MS20995C-.020 wire. Install nut, P&W p/n 30105566 washers each side of rod end bearing, & cotter.
  5. With Power Lever at Ground idle adjust power cable to position control cam shaft on point of propeller control cam.
  6. Adjust FCU rod from propeller control cam for preliminary idle setting on FCU.
  7. Tighten all check nuts and rod ends. Safety wire where required and cotter all castle nuts.
  8. Make any other adjustments on flight line after first run of engine.
