Details of the bottom-load plumbing are shown in Figure 65. Bottom load valves are 3-inch and are located on both sides of the aircraft. These are Agrinautics p/n 79410 and 79450. The aft half of the bottom load plumbing is a 3-inch “Y” assembly and is p/n 80558-1. The forward tube is p/n 80555-1.

A 3-inch hose adapter p/n 79406 is each attached to each. The valves are attached to a bracket inside the fuselage with AN4C10A bolts. A p/n 70533-1 bracket supports the forward tube that attaches the support bracket are QS200M22S.
