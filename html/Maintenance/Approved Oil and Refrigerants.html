R134a Systems

All new production aircraft are equipped with R134a systems. See below for refrigerant and oil quantity.

AT-802/802A System Refrigerant Requirement

Single Condenser System Dual Condenser System
Oil 10.5 oz (310cc) 11.8 oz (349 cc)
Refrigerant 3 lbs (1.36 kg) 3 lbs 3 oz (1.45 kg)

R-12 Systems

Compressor clutches and compressor-clutch magnets are no longer available from the manufacturer for the original R-12 air conditioner system. Since there are still many of these units in service, Air Tractor has an alternate clutch available. The original R-12 clutch is easily identified by the double "V" belt pulley grooves. To install the P/N 25-8502-24 clutch on your aircraft, refer to Service Letter #181.

If your R-12 air conditioning system requires servicing, we suggest that you convert the system to R-134a refrigerant according to Service Letter #159.
