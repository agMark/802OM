1. Ensure battery switch (BATT) is OFF.
  2. Turn fuel valve selector lever to OFF.
  3. Remove engine side cowlings, top cowling, and bottom cowling.
  4. Disconnect batteries.
  5. Remove exhaust stacks. Cover engine exhaust ducts to prevent FOD from entering engine.
  6. Disconnect the engine beta linkage and carbon block assembly from the beta ring.
  7. Remove propeller. (Refer to appropriate Hartzell Owner's Manual). Removal will require the use of a Beta Ring Puller as described in the Hartzell Owner's Manual.
