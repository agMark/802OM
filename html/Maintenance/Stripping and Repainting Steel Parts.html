Steel parts are to be cleaned of all oils and then sand-blasted. If sand-blasting is not available, clean the part thoroughly, wipe dry, sand, and etch with a mixture of 1.0 gal. Isopropyl Alcohol and 1/2 fluid oz. of concentrated Phosphoric acid. If applying finish coat over bare metal, prime first with High Solids Epoxy Primer (see above) and paint using Urethane Topcoat process (see above).
