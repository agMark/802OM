For serial numbers -0001 through -0007, the fuel valve which is located in the lower fuselage area below the cockpit is either a Hallett valve or an Aero Supply valve. They are very similar in appearance, but the Hallett valves have the name HALLETT embossed on the lower side of the valve.

The Aero Supply cam is of a much tougher material, is thicker, and is attached to the shaft with splines and a roll pin. We feel that the Aero Supply cam offers much more resistance to failure than does the Hallett cam. If your aircraft is equipped with a Hallett fuel valve the factory will replace it with an Aero Supply fuel valve per Service Letter #154.
