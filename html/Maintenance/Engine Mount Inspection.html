These areas shown should be visually inspected by a certified mechanic. To perform the inspection, the following steps should be taken:

  1. Remove engine cowling and plenum covers.
  2. Inspect engine mount around all tubing clusters where they attach to the engine mount ring.
  3. Thoroughly check all around every cluster, using FAA Advisory Circular AC 43.13-1B, Chapter 5, Section 2 as a general guide for proper visual inspection techniques.
  4. After inspection, make a notation in aircraft logbooks to indicate that a visual inspection has been completed.
