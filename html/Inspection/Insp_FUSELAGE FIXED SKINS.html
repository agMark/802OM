FFS1 (100) Inspect dispersal equipment boots at fuselage skin attachments for wear.
FFS2 (100) Inspect for skins chafing on other parts, such as the vertical fin, or in the area around the tail spring.
FFS3 (12 Months) Tighten all screws attaching the fixed skins. Look for elongated holes in the angles if loose screws are found.
FFS4 (12 Months) Inspect for cracks or corrosion on all fixed skins. A corroded part should be replaced or else stripped and repainted as described in the Maintenance Section, STRIPPING AND REPAINTING PARTS.
