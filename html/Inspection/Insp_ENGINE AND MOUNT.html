EM1 (100) Remove side cowling and examine exhaust pipe connections and check for cracks.
EM2 (100) Check security of fuel and oil lines and controls at all connections. Inspect for fretting/chafing of all lines and controls.
EM3 (100) Verify that the routing of the flexible Py line does not allow rubbing or chafing on any rotating component or other structure. (Ref. SL327)
EM4 (100) Check controls on primary governor and controls on fuel control. Have someone in cockpit move start lever and then power lever through range of travel and look for loose connections, binding parts, rubbing, etc. CAUTION: Do not move power lever into reverse position with engine stopped or controls will be damaged.
EM5 (100) Inspect engine oil scavenge tubes and fuel lines at engine firewalls for signs of chafing. (Ref. SL126).
EM6 (100) Check the swivel seals/o-rings on propeller, power, fuel condition/shutoff, FCU override, engine alternate air, cockpit heat and auxiliary fuel cables for cracking or deterioration. (Ref. SL404)
EM101 (300) Inspect all cowling parts for cracks, especially at screw connections and in the corners of cut-outs.
EM102 (300) Inspect engine vibration insulators for cracks, swelling, or separation of elastomer.
EM103 (300) Examine engine mount for cracks and corrosion per , and check torque at firewall connections. Torque is 4000 in-lbs on head of upper bolts and 1100-1300 in-lbs on nuts of lower bolts. Reinstall safety wire and cotter pins. (Ref. SL253, SL299 and SL335)
EM104 (300) Inspect FCU to fuel nozzle manifold tubing for chafing. (S/N -0001- through -0073). (Ref. SL173)
EM105 (300) Inspect ITT wires and connectors.
EM501 (12 Months) Remove and inspect P3 filter.
EM502 (12 Months) Inspect SCEET tubes for delamination (as equipped: specifically starter/generator cooling). (Ref. SL397)
