FF1 (100) Check for cracks in upper and lower aft fuselage structure, especially upper aft L/H longeron per S/L #195 and S/L #217A. Check for cracks around tail spring attach area. Check for cracks around main gear attach area.
FF101 (300) Check condition of paint and re-finish where necessary.
FF501 (12 Months) Inspect engine mount attach bushings and surrounding tubes for cracks. Inspect area around wing attach bushings for cracks.
FF502 (12 Months) Inspect all other highly stressed areas for cracks such as rear spar fittings, skin attach brackets, spray boom attach tubes, flap control attachments, control stop brackets, etc.
FF503 (12 Months) Conduct general inspection of entire fuselage frame. Check for cracks and corrosion.
