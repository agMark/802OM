W1 (300) Remove inspection covers and check for cracks in ribs or spar webs.
W2 (300) Inspect wing skins for cracks, loose rivets, and for corrosion. Keep clean.
W3 (12 Months) Inspect wing attach angles for wear, cracks or missing nuts.
W4 (1,000) Remove and inspect the rear spar bolt per instructions in the Maintenance Section, WING ATTACHMENT TO FUSELAGE.
W5 (2,000) Inspect the wing center splice connection as described in the Maintenance Section, WING CENTER SPLICE CONNECTION.
W6 (2000) Loosen (one at a time) the four AN6 bolts through the wing attach angles and rotate ¼ turn to insure that they are not frozen. (See Section 2 – WING ATTACHMENT TO FUSELAGE).
