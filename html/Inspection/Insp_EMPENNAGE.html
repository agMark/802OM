E1 (100) Check vertical fin front spar attach bolt torque at fuselage fittings (420 inch-pounds at nut).
E2 (300) Inspect vertical fin rear spar for cracks near top longeron connection.
E3 (100) Check stabilizers and vertical fin for skin cracks, loose rivets, cracks in spars, ribs, corrosion.
E4 (100) Check trim tab attachments to elevators. Check horn.
E5 (100) Check Neoprene washer at elevator down stop. (See Maintenance Section, ELEVATORS.)
E6 (100) Check stabilizer attaching pedestal for cracks per S/L #180A. When the replacement pedestal is installed, inspections may be performed on a 12-month basis.
E7 (100) Inspect for corrosion on internal horizontal stabilizer braces per S/L #98.
E8 (300) Check torque on all bolts connecting rudder and elevators to fixed surfaces and at elevator horn connection to pedestal and push-rod. Torque should be 100 inch-pounds at the nut.
E9 (12 Months) Inspect horizontal stabilizer struts and fittings for corrosion, erosion, and cracking as referenced in S/L #198. Check bolt torque and tighten check nuts. (See Maintenance Section, STABILIZER RIGGING).
E10 (12 Months) Check torque of stabilizer eye-bolts. Torque of AN47 eyebolts is 600 in-lbs. Torque for 9/16" diameter p/n 30775-1 eye-bolts is 100 ft-lbs.
E11 (12 Months) Inspect all elevator and rudder hinges for wear, loose hardware, and cracking.
E12 (12 Months) Inspect bushings and bolts through rudder horn for wear.
E13 (12 Months) Check control surface travel: Elevators 29° up, 15° down. Rudder travel 24° R. or L. All travels +/- 1°.
E14 (12 Months) Check elevator trim tab travel: With nose full DOWN Trim, Tab at 8° UP from centered (+/- 1 ½ °) (Tab Trailing Edge UP). With nose full UP Trim, Tab at 11° DOWN from centered (+/- 1 ½ °) (Tab Trailing Edge DOWN) For Department of State aircraft, travel is 11° UP, 8° DOWN +/- 1°.
E15 (12 Months) Remove gap covers from stabilizers and check torque on bolts attaching stabilizers to fuselage. (See Maintenance Section, HORIZONTAL STABILIZERS.)
E16 (1350) For planes through s/n 802A-0188, replace eyebolts in stabilizer at strut fittings per S/L #129.
E17 (5000) For planes s/n 802A-0189 and subs, and those modified IAW SL#129A, replace eyebolts in stabilizer at strut fittings per S/L #129.
E18 (100/12 Months) Check the upper rudder hinge and surrounding structure for movement of the upper hinge relative to the rudder, sheared rivets or cracks in the skins, spars, hinge brackets or doublers per Service Letter #247 and Service Letter #331.
E19 (100/12 Months) Inspect all tubes and tube clusters in the immediate vicinity of the vertical and horizontal stabilizer, especially the upper diagonal tube (p/n 11029-10) for cracking (float-equipped aircraft prior to serial number -0259). Repairs to the upper diagonal tube can be performed according to data located in Service Letter #266.
E20 (100) Visual inspection of the horizontal stabilizer auxiliary fin forward attachment (S/N -0001 through -0178) per Service Letter #232.
E21 (earliest of 1000 hours or 12 months) Visual inspection of the horizontal stabilizer auxiliary fin forward attachment (S/N -0179 and subsequent) per Service Letter #232.
