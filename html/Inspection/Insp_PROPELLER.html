P1 (100) Examine all exterior parts of propeller blades for corrosion, nicks, bends, etc.
P2 (100) Remove engine cowling and spinner and inspect for signs of fuel and oil leaks, loose screws, cracks, security of lines and controls, fretting and chafing, etc.
P3 (100) Reinstall spinner and examine spinner installation for loose screws, cracks.
P4 (100) Check Propeller Overspeed Governor and circuit per AFM, Propeller Overspeed Check Procedure.
P5 (100) Inspect and clean beta-block and beta-ring.
P7 (120) Check the torque of the propeller mounting bolts per Hartzell Alert Service Bulletin A203. (Ref SL126C)
P8 (1000) Dynamic-balance propeller.
P9 (12 Months) If equipped with Woodward 8210 Propeller Governor, Inspect and clean beta valve plunger assembly. (Ref. SL172)
P10 (---) Refer to propeller maintenance manual for other inspections.
