OS1 (100) Check oil cooler mounting and check fittings for leaks.
OS2 (300) Check oil hoses for chafing or leaks.
OS3 (100) Turn master switch ON and check Chip Detector Warn Light. Should be OFF. Push to test warn light. If light is ON initially, remove chip detector(s), inspect, clean and reinstall. Perform operational check of Chip Indicating System.
OS4 (100) Inspect for accessory drive drain line oil leaks per Pratt and Whitney Service Bulletins A14458 and 14468 (-67F prior to serial number RZ0090, excluding RZ0079). (Ref SL307)
OS101 (300) Check breather line on accessory gear box for leaks.
OS102 (300) Remove and clean chip detectors.
OS103 (300) Remove and clean oil screen.
OS104 (---) For oil change recommendations, refer to the applicable Pratt and Whitney Service Bulletin.
OS105 (---) Make all other oil system checks as called out in the Engine Maintenance Manual.
