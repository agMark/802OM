ES1 (100) Check batteries for condition, charge, and water level.
ES2 (100) Inspect wires and under battery box and along engine mount tubes for chafing or loose connections.
ES3 (100) Inspect relays and other electrical components in vicinity of battery box and on firewall for loose connections, signs of shorts, corrosion, etc.
ES4 (300) Inspect starter-generator connections to engine, and wiring to terminals.
ES5 (300) Remove R/H side skin below hopper and check wire bundle for security and chafing. Check for chafing at firewall pass-throughs.
ES6 (100) Inspect under instrument panel for loose wires or chafing or evidence of shorting. (It may be necessary to remove the control stick for comfort).
ES7 (100) Turn on master switch (Batt) and check all external and internal lights. Check rheostats.
ES8 (100) Turn on master switch (Batt) and push to test all caution/warning lights.
ES9 (200-300) Inspect and clean the starter generator and brushes per Service Letter #212 .
