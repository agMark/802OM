DE1 (100) Check gate box seal for leaks, check bottom load flange on gate box for leaks, check gate box attachment to hopper for leaks.
DE2 (100) Check condition of hopper lid gasket. Replace if required.
DE3 (100) Check spray pump strut over-center latches for tension and safety position.
DE4 (100) Check to see if plumbing on discharge side of pump is safety wired to top of pump.
DE5 (100) Check bottom-loading plumbing connection to control valve as loose clamps will blow off plumbing here.
DE6 (100) Check boom attach arms for security. Check boom connections to boom Center Section for leaks.
DE7 (100) Inspect all quick-couplers to see if coupler arms are safety wired together.
DE8 (100) Clean strainer screen and check O-ring seal.
DE9 (100) Check control valve for leaks or loose ball.
DE10 (100) Check all hoses for deterioration. Tighten all hose clamps.
DE11 (100) Check for loose hex nipples and loose nozzles in booms.
DE12 (100) Check condition of boom pressure gauge, tubing, and connection to gauge and boom.
DE13 (100) Check fan brake lever for proper adjustment. Rotate fan with brake off to check for drag.
DE14 (100) Check fan brake for free play between the brake hub and the pump shaft. Ideally there should be no free play. If free play is more than a small detectable amount, inspect the key and keyway for wear.
DE15 (100) Check bottom load valve for leaks. Remove shims if worn.
DE16 (12 Months) Unplug digital dispersal monitoring gauge, clean contacts with electrical contact cleaner, and reassemble with Dow Corning 4 Electrical Insulating compound to prevent water entry into plug. Ensure connectors are locked tightly together upon reassembly.
DE17 (12 Months) Check to be sure the lower gate torque tube arm is positioned so as not to chafe the boot or boot ring in the lower fuselage skin.
DE18 (12 Months) Check all wiring for routing, security, chafing, or pulling.
DE19 (12 Months) Ensure hopper float slides easily along the entire length of stainless steel probe.
DE20 (12 Months) Inspect for leaks in boom pressure line.
DE22 (100) Inspect hopper rinse tank shelf attach angles (S/N -0001 through -0219) per Service Letter #248.
