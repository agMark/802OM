The Pratt & Whitney PT6A engine has several life limited components. P&W Service Bulletin 3002 Revision No. 19, or later revision, describes the life limited components, and their limits for the PT6A-45 series engines. Service Bulletin 13002 Revision No. 9, or later revision, describes the life limited components, and their limits for the PT6A-65 series engines. Service Bulletin 14002 Revision No. 5, or later revision, describes the life limited components, and their limits for the PT6A-67 series engines. As outline in these service bulletins operators are required to keep records of accumulated total cycles and all starts and flights for their engines to determine when the service life of life limited components has expired.
