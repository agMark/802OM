The Electronics International MVP-50T Engine Monitor is optionally installed and replaces the following instruments:

  • ITT Gauge
  • Torque-Meter
  • Gas Generator Tachometer (NG)
  • Propeller Tachometer (NP)
  • Fuel Pressure Gauge
  • Oil Pressure Gauge
  • Fuel Quantity Gauge
  • Volt-Ammeter

The following indicator lights are also incorporated into the system:

  • Chip Detect
  • Beta
  • Air Filter
  • Fuel Filter
