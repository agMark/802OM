The propeller used with the PT6A--67AG engine is the Hartzell HC-B5MA-3D/M11691NS five-blade 118" diameter constant-speed with reversing capabilities. The propeller used with the PT6A-65 series engines is the Hartzell HC-B5MP-3F/M11276NS five-blade 115" diameter. The primary propeller governor is furnished with the engine. The over-speed governor is a Woodward p/n 210960 for the -67/-65 and -45R engines. The over-speed governor takes over automatically in case of failure of the primary governor and reduces RPM to 1570 +/- 60 RPM.
