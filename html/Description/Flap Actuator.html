The flap actuator assembly is either an Air Tractor p/n 71112-1 or a Commercial Aircraft Products p/n C100168-4. To determine which actuator is installed, check the size of the ball screw shaft with the actuator deflected. The C100168-4 actuator has a shaft diameter of approximately 9/16". The 71112-1 actuator has a shaft diameter of approximately 1.0". Details of the flap actuator are shown in Fig Cross Reference (C100168-4 actuator shown, 71112-1 actuator similar).
